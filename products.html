Our Products

Explore our wide range of high-quality engineering products tailored to meet diverse industrial needs.

Press Tools

  • Blanking Tools - Used for cutting flat shapes from sheet metal with precision.
  • Progressive Tools - Enable multiple operations in a single press stroke for efficiency.
  • Draw Tools - Designed for shaping sheet metal into hollow or cylindrical forms.
  • High Speed Stamping Tools - Ideal for mass production with high-speed presses.
  • Fine Blanking Tools - Provide superior edge quality and precision for intricate parts.

Mould Tools

  • Injection Moulding Tools - Used for creating plastic parts by injecting molten material into a mould.
  • Compression Moulding Tools - Suitable for shaping thermosetting plastics under heat and pressure.
  • Blow Moulding Tools - Designed for producing hollow plastic parts like bottles and containers.

Gauges

  • Ring Gauges - Used for checking the external dimensions of cylindrical parts.
  • Plug Gauges - Ensure the internal dimensions of holes meet specifications.
  • Air Gauges - Provide non-contact measurement for high-precision applications.
  • Snap Gauges - Quickly verify the dimensions of parts during production.
  • Custom Gauges - Tailored to meet specific measurement requirements.

CNC Machined Parts

  • CNC Milling Parts - Precision-machined components for various industries.
  • Wire EDM Parts - Used for cutting intricate shapes with electrical discharge machining.
  • Honing and Broaching Parts - Provide smooth finishes and precise internal shapes.

Fabrication

  • Laser Cutting Parts - Deliver accurate and clean cuts for complex designs.
  • High Precision Welded Parts - Ensure strength and durability in welded assemblies.
  • Waterjet Cutting Parts - Use high-pressure water for cutting without heat distortion.
  • CNC Turret Punched Parts - Offer precise punching for sheet metal fabrication.
  • CNC Routed Parts - Provide high-quality cuts for wood, plastic, and composites.

Automation

  • Custom Conveyors - Designed to streamline material handling in production lines.
  • Custom Industrial Automation - Tailored solutions to optimize manufacturing processes.

Custom Solutions
